What I Have

by Roswell Asonibe

View

My Creative Work...

One

I best not try to predict what I will make, before I make it.

Two

I have found that What I Have, and what Earth gives, will beget What I Make.

Three

To myself, I say - "Look to what you have done; see the beauty of it."

Four

"Do not worry over the nature of what you will accomplish in the future. Do not ask if it will be good enough. Do not crave for it either!"

Five

"What you and the Unknown make now will determine what you will have later.

And thus, what you will have later... is yours to shape now."

Six

I think of personal events as flowing in this manner:

What I've done -> What I have now (my resource) -> What I can do with my resource -> What I will have done.

"Accept the uncertainty of what you will create and flow."

Seven

"Flow and notice how the Earth's flow, another person's flow - an OverFlow - will spil over and fill your subjective gaps and holes."

"The off-spring of this process is experienced as a deep, satisfying drink...a drink of one's life - multiplied by endless possiblilities."
